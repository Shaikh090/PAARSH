Full-stack development refers to the development of both the front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) portions of a web application.A full-stack developer is skilled in all layers of development,from the user interface to the database.

The front-end is the part of the application that users interact with.It involves everything that users experience on their browser or device.

The back-end consists of the server, databases, and application logic that handle requests from the front-end and process data.
